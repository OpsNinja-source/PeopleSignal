How is this scored?
Every process starts at 100 points. Points are deducted across 6 weighted categories based on what's actually described — not guessed.
Manual Touchpoints
−25 max
Every time a human has to act — send an email, update a sheet, create a ticket, follow up, move data by hand.
System Handoffs
−20 max
Every time information moves between different tools or systems. Each handoff is a place things can break.
Failure Risk
−20 max
Manual reminders required, single-person dependency, email used as the source of truth, no audit trail.
Time Cost
−15 max
Estimated monthly hours of human effort the process consumes.
Scalability
−10 max
Manual assignment, manual follow-up, manual status tracking — would this survive the team doubling overnight?
Compliance & Auditability
−10 max
No audit trail, manual approval tracking, or data living in email rather than a system of record.
